Mar 2023
  • Breath-by-breath data was used by the Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) recurrent neural network model with inputs of absolute (VO2) and relative (VO2/kg) oxygen uptake, carbon dioxide production (VCO2), and minute ventilation (VE).
  • The network output produced a 1x3 vector specifying the probability of the point to lie in the first region (input < VT1), second region (VT1 < input < VT2), or the third region (input > VT2).
  • The LSTM model produced an accuracy of 99.7% with a categorical loss of the model was 0.002.
  • Prediction accuracy of VT1 was 92% and for VT2 was 97% and the MAE between the predicted VT1 and ground truth VT1 was 3.22 ml/kg/min and between predicted VT2 and ground truth VT2 was 3.12 ml/kg/min.
